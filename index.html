Quantifying the interpolation uncertainty of radiosonde humidity profiles

Measurement Science and Technology, Vol. 33 (7), 074001 (12 pp) — April 2022

Investigates the uncertainty introduced when filling gaps in radiosonde relative-humidity profiles. Compares linear vs Gaussian-process interpolation, considering interpolation distance (in seconds), altitude, and launch site.

Results: Produced a lookup table that associated interpolation uncertainty to gap length, altitude and launch site. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}
